Welcome to Defenceology

Defenceology emerges from the union of "defence" and "-ology," where "-ology" signifies the study or science of something. Hence, "Defenceology" encapsulates the systematic exploration of military strategies, technologies, and the intricate art of safeguarding nations. It delves deep into the realms where strength meets strategy, and where every move is a calculated step in the symphony of global security.
